Overview

CleanSpace is a photo and storage management app designed to help users identify and remove unwanted photos and videos from their device.

Data Collection

CleanSpace does not collect, store, or transmit personal data to external servers.

The app processes photo and storage information locally on your device only. No photos, videos, or personal content leave your device.

Photos Access

CleanSpace requires access to your Photos library in order to scan and identify duplicate photos, screenshots, large videos, and similar items.

All scanning and analysis happens entirely on-device.

Subscriptions

CleanSpace offers an optional Ad-Free subscription. Payments are processed securely through Apple. CleanSpace does not have access to your Apple ID or payment information.
